Why Don’t Other Programs Work Like the 28-Day Keto Challenge?

When taking on other keto diets, most people don’t plan to have an end in sight. When the plan is just to continue with the keto diet until the user loses weight, this method can take months and months. Most people don’t stick with a diet this long, and even the smallest amount of carbohydrates will cause the body to revert to the fat-burning methods it used before.

By condensing the regimen into just four weeks, users know exactly how long they have to stick with the program. There’s no guesswork, which means that users won’t have to feel the constant pull to give up. Having a way to carry through one month of a diet gives the user a plan, and over 400,000 people have already seen massive improvements with the program.
